ABOUT US

                   Pringles are available in several flavors. Until the 1980s, only the original flavor was 
                available in the US. Standard flavors in the US as of 2020 include original, salt and vinegar, 
                sour cream and onion, cheddar cheese, ranch dressing, barbecue, hot and spicy, and loaded baked 
                potato. Some flavors are distributed only to limited market areas; for example, prawn cocktail, 
                wasabi, and curry flavors have been available in the United Kingdom and the Republic of Ireland.
